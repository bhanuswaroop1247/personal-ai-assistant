## Where I Want to Go

My long-term direction is at the intersection of **medical technology and applied machine learning** — specifically in computer vision for clinical diagnostics or medical imaging.

Target roles (3–5 year horizon):
- Applied ML Engineer / Computer Vision Engineer – medtech company
- Research Scientist – diagnostic imaging startup
- Data Scientist – ophthalmology device company (Alcon, Zeiss, Topcon, OCULUS)

I am deliberately not targeting pure software engineering roles (no clinical domain) or pure research (no applied impact) — I want both.

## Application Strategy

### Types of Roles to Target
1. **Research Engineer (industry):** Companies with R&D divisions building diagnostic tools
2. **ML Engineer (early-stage medtech startup):** Broader scope, faster learning
3. **Data Scientist (clinical data):** Hospitals, pharma, health informatics

### How to Position Myself
- Not: "I know scikit-learn and pandas"
- Yes: "I built an end-to-end clinical ML system — from feature extraction on medical imaging data to model evaluation with clinically-relevant metrics, targeting publication"

### Portfolio Evidence
- Paper submission (in progress) → strongest signal of depth
- Pupil tracking GitHub repo → evidence of real-world engineering
- Interview prep notes → demonstrates systematic thinking
